# DOMINIUM FAN CONTENT POLICY
## Version 1.0

This policy explains what kinds of fan, commentary, educational, and community content may be created around Dominium without separate written permission.

This policy does not replace `LICENSE.md`, `TRADEMARKS.md`, `CONTRIBUTOR_LICENSE_AGREEMENT.md`, or any separate written agreement from the Project Owner.

---

# 1. BASIC RULE

You may create limited non-commercial fan, commentary, review, educational, or community content about Dominium if You follow this policy and do not copy, distribute, modify, host, monetise, or misrepresent the Software.

This policy allows discussion and limited presentation. It does not allow independent distribution of the game, engine, source code, assets, tools, packs, builds, servers, forks, or derivative works.

---

# 2. PERMITTED NON-COMMERCIAL FAN CONTENT

Subject to this policy, You may create and share non-commercial:

- commentary, reviews, essays, and educational discussion;
- bug reports, compatibility notes, and security writeups;
- screenshots or short clips from official public materials, where those materials have been lawfully released for public viewing;
- personal notes, diagrams, or explanations that discuss the project without copying substantial repository content;
- community guides that link to the Official Repository instead of redistributing project files.

Any use must be accurate, lawful, non-misleading, and clearly unofficial unless the Project Owner expressly says otherwise.

---

# 3. COMMERCIAL AND MONETISED USE

Unless the Project Owner gives prior express written permission, You may not use Dominium fan content, project materials, names, marks, screenshots, clips, assets, repository contents, builds, servers, or derivative works for Commercial Use.

Commercial Use includes, without limitation:

1. selling or licensing fan content;
2. charging for access, downloads, memberships, servers, packs, mods, tools, builds, guides, or marketplaces;
3. using ad-supported, sponsored, paid, subscription, affiliate, or donation-gated distribution where Dominium material is a substantial part of the content;
4. using Dominium material to promote a product, service, brand, studio, tool, platform, or commercial project;
5. using Dominium material in paid courses, paid consulting, commercial training, commercial research, or business workflows.

---

# 4. PROHIBITED FAN CONTENT

You may not, without prior express written permission:

1. redistribute source code, binaries, assets, tools, data, schemas, contracts, packs, fixtures, generated artifacts, or repository contents;
2. publish or distribute modified versions, builds, forks, patches, servers, launchers, mod loaders, packs, products, services, or derivative works;
3. imply official status, endorsement, sponsorship, certification, compatibility, or affiliation;
4. use Dominium or Domino names, logos, marks, icons, visual identity, or confusingly similar branding as the primary identity of Your own project or community;
5. post leaked, private, unreleased, confidential, security-sensitive, or access-controlled material;
6. bypass security, licensing, audit, capability, law, compatibility, or refusal controls;
7. create public confusion about release status, project authority, ownership, security, compatibility, or governance;
8. use project material in unlawful, harassing, deceptive, defamatory, exploitative, or harmful content.

---

# 5. SCREENSHOTS, VIDEO, STREAMING, AND REVIEWS

Until an official public release or preview explicitly opens wider media permissions, screenshots, video, streaming, and review use are limited to material the Project Owner has already made public for viewing.

Do not publish recordings, builds, gameplay footage, UI footage, asset extractions, internal tools, unreleased features, private branches, security-sensitive diagnostics, or repository-derived material unless the Project Owner has expressly allowed that use.

Monetised videos, sponsored streams, ad-supported channels, paid reviews, paid previews, and commercial media use require prior express written permission.

---

# 6. WIKIS, GUIDES, AND COMMUNITY RESOURCES

Unofficial wikis, guides, compatibility notes, and community resources must:

1. state clearly that they are unofficial;
2. link to the Official Repository instead of redistributing project files;
3. avoid copying substantial source code, assets, documentation, schemas, contracts, or generated artifacts;
4. avoid using Dominium or Domino branding as if the resource is official;
5. avoid charging for access or monetising the resource without written permission.

---

# 7. MODS, PACKS, AND SERVERS

This policy does not grant permission to create, distribute, host, publish, monetise, or maintain mods, packs, servers, forks, builds, launchers, mod loaders, tools, or derivative works.

Modding and pack policy is governed by `MODDING.md`, `LICENSE.md`, relevant contracts, and any written permission from the Project Owner.

---

# 8. TAKEDOWN AND REVOCATION

The Project Owner may require removal, correction, disclaimer changes, demonetisation, renaming, or cessation of any fan or community content that violates this policy, creates confusion, misuses project identity, harms the project, or conflicts with legal terms.

The Project Owner may change or revoke this policy for future uses at any time. Existing uses that violate updated terms may need to be corrected or removed where reasonably required.

---

# 9. CONTACT

Requests for commercial fan content, media access, review keys, previews, screenshots, video, streaming, guides, wiki use, compatibility claims, or other permission should be directed through the Official Repository or another contact channel formally published by the Project Owner.
