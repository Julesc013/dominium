# HISTORY VIEWER





History viewing MUST surface timelines, provenance traces, and conflicting narratives.


History MUST be treated as artifacts that may be incomplete or wrong.


Causal explanations MUST be derived from recorded events only; no heuristics.


History viewing MUST be read-only and deterministic.
