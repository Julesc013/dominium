# Realism Is Content

Status: canonical.
Scope: realism boundary and prohibitions.
Authority: canonical. All realism assumptions MUST be externalized.

## Binding rules
- Realism MUST be supplied by content packs and model families.
- The engine MUST be realism-agnostic and MUST NOT privilege any physics.
- Scientific accuracy is optional and replaceable.
- Alternate physics, magic, astrology, and false cosmologies MUST be valid when
  declared by content.

## Explicit prohibitions
- The engine MUST NOT hardcode physical laws, constants, or default cosmology.
- The engine MUST NOT embed a "true science" baseline.
- The engine MUST NOT assume Earth, Sol, humans, or any real-world baseline.
