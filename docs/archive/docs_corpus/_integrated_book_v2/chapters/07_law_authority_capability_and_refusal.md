## 7. Law, Authority, Capability, and Refusal

Authority grants permission to attempt; law decides accept, refuse, transform, or degrade in deterministic, auditable form.

**Why this chapter matters.** Constitutional axioms and AGENTS make explicit refusal, no hidden fallback, and capability/law discipline binding. Archive material supplies examples: Workbench, providers, packs, and assistants must not become hidden truth owners.
