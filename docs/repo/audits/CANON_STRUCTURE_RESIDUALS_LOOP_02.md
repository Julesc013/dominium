## Provider And Third-Party Policy

The pass preserves the rule:

- service roots are first-party identities;
- provider folders are replaceable implementations;
- profiles select providers;
- third-party code and types are fenced;
- apps remain generic and do not hardwire provider identity.

Planned raylib, SDL2, OpenGL, Lua, null, and Dominium asset provider references
are recorded as `support_claim = false`. They may be selected in provisional
profile descriptions, but they do not assert implementation support or create
empty provider directories.
