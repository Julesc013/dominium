# PLAYER NON-GOALS





The player system MUST NOT:


- assume a UI exists


- grant god-mode authority


- bypass epistemic uncertainty


- bypass physics or processes


- special-case human or player agents





Player embodiment MUST be simulation-first, not UI-first.
