# Licensing Strategy

This document is a governance model, not legal advice. Consult counsel before issuing final license texts.

## Model Language

- Engine may be closed-source.
- Game layer may be open or closed independently of the engine.
- Applications may be open, closed, or mixed according to the active governance mode.
- Schemas and protocols remain open specification surfaces.
- Pack format specifications remain open.
- Redistribution rights may differ by artifact kind.

## Redistribution Policy Shape

- Binaries may have distinct redistribution terms from documentation and schemas.
- Official release indices and manifests remain content-addressed and archiveable.
- Third-party ecosystems may publish their own packs and indices under their own governance profile.
- Commercial packs are allowed as policy artifacts; they must still comply with trust and compatibility contracts.
