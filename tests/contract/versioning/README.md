# Versioning And Deprecation Fixtures

These fixtures prove the initial VERSION-DEPRECATION-LAW-01 validator behavior.

Positive fixtures show:

- stable surfaces declaring compatibility range and proof
- deprecation notices with replacement, migration, refusal, and removal policy
- allowed lifecycle transitions with migration/refusal proof

Negative fixtures show:

- deprecation without replacement or no-replacement reason
- removed surfaces without retirement history
- stable surfaces without compatibility range
- breaking changes without major version bump, migration, or refusal policy

The fixtures are contract examples only. They do not deprecate, retire, remove, or migrate any active Dominium surface.
